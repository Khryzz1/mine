The process

Four stages. One standard.

Every tonne of ore follows the same disciplined path from mountain to market.

01

Survey & Geological Planning

LiDAR drones and underground sensors map ore zones to centimetre precision before a single drill bit touches the rock. Deposit models are reviewed by independent geologists before any extraction plan is approved.

02

Drilling & Blasting

Precision drill rigs bore 120mm holes to exact depths. Controlled blasting is executed under seismic monitoring to protect tunnels, surface structures, and nearby communities from ground movement.

03

Haulage & Ore Processing

Electric haul trucks transport blasted rock to crushing facilities. Gravity separation, flotation, and cyanide leaching extract gold and copper concentrates to certified purity grades at our on-site refinery.

04

Secure Logistics & Delivery

Refined product is assayed, hallmarked, and transported by armoured carrier to buyers and central banks. Full traceability documentation accompanies every shipment from mine to vault.
