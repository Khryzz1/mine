Since 1881

Built on Alpine heritage and honest ground

Swiss Mining Co. was founded when prospectors discovered rich quartz veins threading through the Graubünden highlands. What began as a family claim became Switzerland's most respected mineral enterprise.

Today we honour that legacy by maintaining the same transparency and technical rigour that made our founders trusted across Europe's commodity markets.

  • Founded in 1881 in Chur, Graubünden
  • First mechanised Alpine extraction 1923
  • ISO 9001 certified operations since 1998
  • Community partnerships across 12 Alpine villages
